About SJTU

Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) is a prestigious public research university located in Shanghai, China. Founded in 1896, it operates under the direct administration of China’s Ministry of Education and has developed a strong reputation for academic excellence, innovation, and global collaboration. Shanghai Jiao Tong University is a member of the elite C9 League, often referred to as China’s equivalent of the Ivy League, and participates in several major national research initiatives. The university offers a broad range of academic programs across 77 undergraduate disciplines and numerous graduate fields, attracting students from around the world. With modern research facilities, distinguished faculty, and a vibrant international campus environment, SJTU plays a leading role in advancing scientific research, technological development, and global education.
